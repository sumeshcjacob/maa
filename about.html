Ma Dayanitha Foundation

Thanks for taking your time and following MaaDayanitha Global Foundation. We truly appreciate it and heartly welcomes you to our family. we are a charitable trust focusing on spreading the message of HH Ma Dayanitha Devi's love and compassion to those who need it the most. We primarily focus on people from less privileged sections of the society by providing medical assistance to Newborns and elderly, Holistic education for children, Improving maternal Health Access to essential maternity care for vulnerable mothers , providing financial assistance for medical bills, Preventing malnutrition etc. We expect your esteemed cooperation in supporting these causes.

Privacy Policy

Effective January 1, 2020 MDF foundation is committed to safeguarding the privacy of its on-line users' Personal information. Please read the following policy to understand the rules and procedures followed by MDF Foundation in handling Personal information provided to us over the Internet. If you have further questions regarding our privacy policy please contact us via email at maadayanithafoundation@gmail.com. What information does MDF Foundation collect? MDF Foundation collects the Personal information only from persons who contact us and voluntarily provide the information to us or that is given to us by various affiliated MDF organizations (Distribution points and centers) that have your permission to share your Personal information. “Personal information” means your name, shipping/mailing address, telephone number, fax number, email address, credit or debit card account number and may include your retreat registration and donation preferences. How MDF Foundation uses the Personal Information We use your Personal information to respond to and fulfill requests for products and services including: 1. sending information about the activities of Maa Dayanitha Devi, MDF Foundation and our affiliates 2. sending you our e-newsletters 3. contacting you about the status of your orders, donations and retreat registrations 4. completing your purchases and retreat registrations 5. completing your donations When we disclose personal information to others 1. MDF Foundation does exchange Personal information with various affiliated MDF organizations (satsangs and centers) in the India and abroad so that they can contact people on the mailing list about Amma programs and events. 2. MDF Foundation's mailing list, including the Personal information we receive through the Internet, is never shared with any commercial organizations and is never sold, rented or given to unrelated third parties. 3. MDF Foundation will disclose Personal information to third parties if we believe in good faith that the law requires us to do so. Right to control and correct Personal information 1. Individuals who wish to register online for Maa Dayanitha Devi retreats are asked to open an “account” in which they provide a user name and password. Individuals can access their account to change that information during open registration time simply by clicking on “Register online” link. Please contact us at maadayanithafoundation@gmail.com at any other time to change that information. 2. Individuals who wish to receive information about Maa Dayanitha foundation activities via the E-newsletter provide name and email address. Individuals can change the information at any time by emailing us at maadayanithafoundation@gmail.com, to update your preferences 3. Individuals who purchase goods or services from the bookstore or who give donations are asked to supply their Personal information at each transaction. Individuals can change the information at any time by emailing us at maadayanithafoundation@gmail.com How we protect your Personal information 1. MDF Foundation uses a secure server with 128 bit encryption to assure the security of the data being transmitted to MDF Foundation. MDF Foundation's database is protected from unauthorized access by a series of highly sophisticated software protection programs. 2. MDF Foundation secures the Personal information, including credit card numbers, received from individuals who make online purchases from MDF Foundation. Our secure server software provides protection for your Personal information. We encrypt all of your Personal information, including credit card number, name, and address, so that it cannot be read as the information travels over the Internet. This data is used only to confirm our existing records. Sensitive data is also encrypted while being stored on our server. 3. MDF Foundation limits access to your Personal information to those persons that are required to fulfill the requests for information, services or products. Your Personal information is password protected with access only to those selected and authorized persons. 4. MDF Foundation provides links to affiliated entities. These sites operate independently of MDF Foundation. MDF Foundation is not responsible for the privacy, terms of use or other policies of any other web site. How we use Cookies 1. MDF Foundation does use cookies to store information. A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored in your computer's hard drive. 2. MDF Foundation gives the option to the user to store their personal preferences as cookies. These cookies are for your convenience only, and are not used for any other purpose. Amendment of Policy MDF Foundation reserves the right to amend this Privacy Policy any time. No individual notice of such changes will be provided but they will appear on the website in the form of a revised and newly dated Privacy Policy. Our current policy was amended January 1, 2020.
